# Case Study Blurb

## 1-Sentence Version

Built Monthly AI Content Engine, a preset-aware AI content automation platform that turns one business brief into a full month of social content with history, exports, and Google Docs delivery.

## Short Portfolio Version

Monthly AI Content Engine is a productized social content planning system built with Next.js, TypeScript, and a Claude-ready AI architecture. It generates a 30-day content calendar, captions, carousel outlines, short-form video scripts, marketing scripts, hashtags, and image prompts from one structured brief, then packages the output into clean markdown, JSON, HTML, print, and Google Docs exports.

## Job-Application Version

Built a multi-niche AI content automation application with typed schemas, prompt modularity, provider abstraction, local persistence, export workflows, and OAuth-based Google Docs delivery. The app supports demo-safe generation as well as live Claude integration, making it easy to show both product polish and real automation systems thinking.

## Client-Facing Version

Monthly AI Content Engine helps turn a short business brief into a full month of organized social content. Instead of manually planning captions, scripts, and campaign structure each month, the app creates a complete content system that can be reviewed in-app and exported into client-ready delivery formats, including Google Docs.
